About Me

Passionate about creating
digital experiences that matter

Hey, I'm Argyadipta — a Computer Science student at Universitas Negeri Jakarta who genuinely loves the craft of building things on the web. I don't just code to finish assignments; I build because I care about how things look, feel, and work for real people.

My focus is web development and modern digital experiences. I'm drawn to clean interfaces, thoughtful interactions, and code that's actually readable. I teach myself constantly — through side projects, docs, and breaking things until they work the way I want them to.

Beyond web dev, I've been exploring cybersecurity fundamentals — understanding how systems can be made more resilient is something that fascinates me. I see it as another dimension of building responsibly.

I believe the best developers are curious first. I'm still early in this journey, but I'm building, learning, and growing every day.
